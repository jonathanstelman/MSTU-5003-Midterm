Quick Lessons

Learn Ukulele the serious way

Many people treat the ukulele as a fun novelty. We agree that it is a very fun instrument, but it is so much more than a toy. This site is for people who want to not only learn to play a few songs, but also for people who want to become musicians too. Serious musicianship does not belong to only pianists violinists!

Try it for free!

Using the navigation bar at the top, you will find many sample lessons. Serious Ukulele is designed to be accessible to players of all levels. Beginnning players will be able to take advantage of many of many free resources, including instructional videos and history lessons.


Go Premium!

As you go deeper into your exploration of the ukulele and music, consider becoming a premium member so you have access to interactive discussion boards, downloadable reference sheets, and a personalized lesson plan. There's a lot to discover, and Serious Ukulele wants to ensure there is not limit to the depths of your musical journey!
